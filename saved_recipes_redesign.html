Truffle Risotto with Wild Mushrooms

⏱️ 35 mins 👥 4 servings ⭐ Difficulty: Medium 📅 Created 3 days ago
A luxurious Italian risotto featuring black truffle oil, wild porcini mushrooms, and aged Parmigiano-Reggiano. Perfect for special occasions.
Italian Vegetarian Luxury Date Night
Ingredients
  • 300g Arborio rice
  • 200g mixed wild mushrooms (porcini, shiitake)
  • 1.5L warm vegetable stock
  • 2 tbsp black truffle oil
  • 100g Parmigiano-Reggiano, grated
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 150ml dry white wine
Instructions
1. Sauté shallots and garlic until fragrant
2. Add rice and toast for 2 minutes
3. Pour in wine and stir until absorbed
4. Gradually add warm stock, stirring constantly
5. Fold in mushrooms and truffle oil
6. Finish with Parmigiano-Reggiano and serve immediately
